RISC-V Softcore

During my undergraduate studies at Cal Poly, I discovered a true passion for computer architecture and design, particularly through courses like CPE 233 - CPE 333: Computer Design and Assembly Language Programming and Computer Hardware Architecture and Design, respectively. Seeking to apply my knowledge and gain practical experience, I have embarked on an exciting project to create my very own softcore, based on the cutting-edge RISCV architecture. My focus in this endeavor encompasses various essential aspects, including establishing the rv32i base set and thoroughly exploring communication protocols such as I2C and UART. Moreover, I am actively planning to develop a serial programmer that will empower me to effortlessly flash new programs onto the softcore, amplifying its versatility and potential.
